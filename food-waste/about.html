About Us


"Welcome to Food Donate, the largest and most trusted donating platform that connects donors to verified nonprofits in Malaysia. Food Donate helps you become a ray of hope for people in need. Choose a cause that is close to your heart and join hands with millions of donors like you who aim to make this world a better place."

We are a team of passionate individuals committed to addressing the issue of food waste in Malaysia. Our goal is to create a system that connects food donors with charities and NGOs, while also reducing the environmental impact of food waste.
